Our story

Founded in 2014, Biz499 is a crafty team of strategists, designers, developers, and marketers.

We work with people across a wide range of sectors including established brands, non-profits, universities, startups, and entrepreneurs.

Some of our past clientele includes Yourstory, Ungrezi, ExamByte, EduIITians, Befits Marketing Solutions and many more.

We aim to disrupt knowledge and skill based Jobs Industry by offering long term solutions to both businesses and professionals who have or desire to hone in their Design, Development, Marketing and Sales skills.

A marketing agency which aims to disrupt the quality pricing and quality of applying knowledge based skills for making viable businesses and a fruitful career for its employees.

We will provide an environment for our employees which is conducive to learning, creativity and innovation. We will also offer a competitive salary and benefits package. Our goal is to be the premier provider of knowledge based marketing services in the world.

Our mission is to produce better and long term business relationships with knowledge workers and a business's clientele. We aim to provide our clients with the necessary tools and strategies to succeed in today's highly competitive marketplace!

In addition, we strive to create an environment that is conducive to cooperation and collaboration between businesses and knowledge workers.

We uphold the following values: creativity, discipline, innovation, and teamwork. These values should be evident in every aspect of the our agency’s work, from the way we conceives and executes campaigns to the way we manage our internal operations!

By adhering to these values, we set ourselves apart from the competition and build a strong reputation with clients and employees alike.
